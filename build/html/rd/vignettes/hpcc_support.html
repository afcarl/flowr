As of now we have tested this on the following clusters:

Platform command status queue.type
LSF 7 bsub Not tested lsf
LSF 9.1 bsub Yes lsf
Torque qsub Yes torque
SGE qsub Beta sge

*queue short-name used in `flow <https://github.com/sahilseth/flow>>`__

There are several job scheduling systems available and we try to support the major players. Adding support is quite easy if we have access to them. Your favourite not in the list? Send a message

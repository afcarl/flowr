flow

R pacakge to design (and submit) complex workflows, such that optimization/parallelization is easy.

Here is a shiny app, flow_creator for you start building your shiny flow. [guest; guestpass]

The process of submitting jobs uses the dependency feature of submitting jobs to a computing cluster. This lets the user concentrate more on the type of analysis than its implmentation. Also the pipeline becomes really portable across platforms and institutions.

Just get me started...

install.packages('devtools')
devtools:::install_github("sahilseth/flow")
require(flow)
qobj <- queue(type="lsf", queue="normal")
jobj1 <- job()
jobj2 <- job()
fobj <- flow(jobs = list(jobj1, jobj2)))
